üîë Permissions Explained

SnapKeep requests minimal permissions for core functionality:

activeTab

Required to capture screenshots of the currently active tab only when you explicitly request it. This is more privacy-friendly than requesting access to all tabs.

contextMenus

Adds right-click menu options for easy access to screenshot features without needing additional UI elements.

storage

Saves your screenshots and settings locally on your device only. No data is ever transmitted to external servers.

scripting

Creates the temporary area selection overlay when you choose to capture a specific region. Only injects minimal UI code, never accesses or modifies page data.

offscreen

Processes images for area selection cropping in a secure, isolated environment without accessing page content.

üë§ Your Rights and Control
